Reliable Casting Agency

The Casting Agency models a company that is responsible for creating movies and managing and assigning actors to those movies. This is a system provides an API to keep track of Movies and Actors to simplify and streamline the process.

The application uses Auth0 for authenticating and authorizing users. Refer to below for various roles and sample login credentials to explore the application.
